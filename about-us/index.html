About Kiriin Resources

Kiriin Resources is a Sydney based construction company creating bespoke architectural homes. Fuelled by an endless desire to shoot for perfection our focus is to consistently produce builds that are to the highest standard, with an emphasis on collaboration and detail.

We operate the business based on our three core values honesty, professionalism and teamwork; these fundamental principles are always at the forefront of what we do.

At BIC, we’re about building relationships with our clients and partners that are open, collaborative and formed on trust. These relationships ensure that the journey to a creating a beautiful home is a happy one.

Bringing creative and innovative designs to life is what we are all about! The kind of home that makes everyone – clients, architects and the guy walking his dog down the street – turn around and say “What an amazing house.”

Brad Chapman

Director

In 2013 my wife and I welcomed a daughter, it was this defining change that pushed my desire to start my own business.

My passion lies within the small details of building, by nature I have always been a perfectionist. I love my craft and what I do which is why my dream was to establish an exceptional and innovative building company that creates remarkable architectural homes.

On an architectural build there is not only challenging detail, but the opportunity to create something beautiful on a grand scale. I love poring over architect’s plans, visualising every detail of how we’re going to put a build together.

We have worked hard, growing from building small residential projects into the amazing architectural construction company that it is today.

I will continue to push towards growing and expanding the company, and will continually strive to improve and educate myself to ensure that BIC represents my dream.

Core Values

At the heart of Kiriin Resources are our three core values

Professionalism
  • We don’t see anything as being impossible; we simply see opportunities to innovate. Sometimes this means thinking outside the box, perhaps using unique materials or fresh methods to create inspiring outcomes.
  • We believe quality is a mindset and that the best outcomes are the result of a quality approach.
  • We take care of the basics – we appreciate the importance of reliability, punctuality, personal presentation and communicating in a professional manner.
Teamwork
  • Our aim is to form solid long-lasting relationships with great architects, to work professionally and cohesively alongside each other, and for all parties including our clients to enjoy the journey of creating a remarkable home.
  • Great outcomes begin with effective collaboration. A clear flow of communication between the client, builder and architect is key to great results in all areas of the build process. Given we’ll be your “man on the ground”, we’ll make sure our communication is crystal clear and consistent.
Honesty
  • Building a home is one of the biggest endeavours a person can undertake, both emotionally and financially. It’s vital that all parties involved feel secure with their chosen builder. We believe in working together in an open and honest manner so that everyone feels informed and confident throughout the build process.

Brad Chapman

Director

    My first love is my family followed closely by my company. While both can be challenging and tiring, the joy and reward that comes from a child that scored their first goal or a project which has exceeded expectations is strangely similar. This may seem a little odd comparing your family with your company but I guess you could say that Kiriin Resources is a third child to myself and my wife Mikala.

    The journey began when I entered the construction industry in 2000, obtaining my carpentry licence 4 years later. As a subcontract carpenter, I gained experience working on high end residential projects while furthering my education completing the certificate IV in construction management. In 2013 we welcomed a daughter, it was this defining change that pushed my desire to start my own business.

    We have worked hard, growing from building small residential projects into the amazing architectural construction company that it is today.

    I will continue to push towards growing and expanding the company, and will continually strive to improve and educate myself to ensure that BIC represents my dream.

    A dream of creating a successful innovative building company that produces exceptional projects in a manner that our clients are beyond happy with.
